Сopyright

Any photo taken by a professional or amateur taken with a high-tech camera or the eye of a mobile phone is protected by copyright on the basis of Article 1265 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation (Civil Code). Photography refers to works of art in the same way as painting, graphics, sculpture, drawing, caricature, comic book, design sketch, layout, etc., and its author has all the property and non-property rights to this object just because it created it.
